What We Do

Pure and Clean Window Cleaners began in 1983 as Fosters Cleaners, using traditional Window Cleaning techniques. We provide Commercial and Domestic Window cleaning services for North Staffordshire and South Cheshire. We also provide yearly maintenance plans which include gutter cleaning and conservatory roof cleaning.
